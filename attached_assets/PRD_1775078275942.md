Product Requirements Document: Page Pace Reading Habit Survey
Overview

Build a short web-based survey targeted at college students that collects information about their personal reading habits, distractions, and consistency challenges. The app has two pages: a survey form and a results page showing aggregated responses. Responses are stored in a Supabase PostgreSQL database.

The purpose of this survey is to understand why students struggle to maintain consistent reading habits, especially during end-of-day burnout periods.

Tech Stack
Framework: Vite + React
Database: Supabase (PostgreSQL)
Hosting: Azure Static Web Apps
Charting: Recharts
Survey Questions

The survey must include the following seven questions, using the specified input types:

#	Question	Input Type
1	How often do you read for personal enjoyment?	Radio buttons
2	When do you usually plan to read?	Dropdown list
3	What distracts you from reading?	Checkboxes
4	How long do you typically read?	Dropdown list
5	How do you feel when you skip reading?	Radio buttons
6	What helps you stay consistent?	Checkboxes
7	Why do you struggle to read consistently?	Text input
